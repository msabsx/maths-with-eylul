FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS.

What age groups do you teach?
+

Ages 7 right through to adults of any age — primary, KS3, GCSE, and adult learners returning to education. No minimum or maximum.

Is the app really free?
+

Yes — 3 daily challenges, 5 bot questions, and 1 mock exam per month completely free. No card required. Premium unlocks everything for £4.99/month.

How does the AI Maths Bot work?
+

Powered by advanced AI, it can answer any maths question, show step-by-step working, give hints, and remember context throughout your session.

Do I need the app AND lessons?
+

No — they work independently. The app is great for daily practice. The 1-to-1 lessons are for personalised support. Many students use both together.

What if I need to cancel a lesson?
+

Just message via WhatsApp. Block bookings don't expire so you never lose sessions. I'm flexible and understanding.

How quickly will I see results?
+

Most students notice a confidence boost within the first few sessions. GCSE students have improved by 2–3 grades with consistent lessons.

Are lessons available in person near me?
+

In-person lessons are available in Liverpool, Manchester, North West and Cheshire. Online lessons are available anywhere in the UK via Google Meet or Zoom.

Are payments secure?
+

All payments are processed securely via Stripe. We never store your card details. All transactions are SSL encrypted and PCI compliant.
